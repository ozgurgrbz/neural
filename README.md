# NEURAL: AI Build Battle

A competitive card game where you race to build a complete AI system before your opponent dismantles it.

**Play it live:** [https://ozgurgrbz.github.io/neural-game](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/neural-game)

## How to Play

- Fill all 4 Build Zone slots (Foundation, Intelligence, Deployment, Alignment)
- Reach a combined Power Score of 20+
- Survive one Survival Round
- OR assemble all 4 Kings for an instant Singularity Victory

## Cards

54 cards across 4 suits + 2 Jokers:
- ♠ Foundation — How AI learns
- ♥ Intelligence — How AI thinks
- ♦ Deployment — How AI acts
- ♣ Alignment — How AI relates to humans

## Tech

Pure HTML/CSS/JS — no dependencies, no build step.
