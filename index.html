HOW TO PLAY

Objective: Fill all 4 Build Zone slots with a combined Power Score of 20+, then survive 1 Survival Round.

On your turn, take 2 actions:
DRAW — Take a card from the deck (max 9 in hand)
BUILD — Select a card, click Build, then click a matching slot
ATTACK — Select an attack card, click Attack, choose opponent's slot
DEFEND — Only available when AI attacks you

Suits: ♠ Foundation (blue) · ♥ Intelligence (red) · ♦ Deployment (green) · ♣ Alignment (purple)

Combos: Assemble both cards of a pair in your Build Zone for a bonus + 1 Stability Token.

⚡ HALLUCINATION: Removes any card regardless of power. Returns to deck after use.

🔥 BREAKTHROUGH: Attaches to any Build Zone card. +2 Power permanently.

Stability Tokens: Spend 2 to block an attack without a defense card.
